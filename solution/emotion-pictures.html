Solution: Emotion Pictures
Answer: REDHEAD

Written by Anderson Wang

Other than the final image, each of the other 7 iMessage screenshots consists of the same four things:

  1. A few emojis at the top
  2. A year at the top
  3. A line of several emojis
  4. A single emoji followed by a series of question marks. The emoji always appears in (3)

The first step is to realize that each of these screenshots clues a movie, which is hinted at by the title (“Emotion Pictures” is one letter off from “motion pictures”). (1) represents the title of the movie, sometimes in a punny way, (2) is the release year, (3) represents an emoji plot overview of the movie, and (4) singles out a character from the movie. The number of question marks always matches the enumeration of the character’s name in (4), and the screenshots are ordered alphabetically by movie title.

Title Year Emoji plot explanation Character
21 Jump Street 2012 Two cops (Morton, Greg) go to a school, where a popular student (Eric) is supplying drugs. At the end it’s revealed that a teacher (Mr. Walters) was the mastermind behind everything. MR WALTERS
Bridesmaids 2011 A bride (Lillian) invites various friends to be bridesmaids, including a baker (Annie) and a rich woman (Helen). Annie has a romantic relationship with a police officer (Rhodes). LILLIAN DONOVAN
Chef 2014 A chef (Carl) starts up a food truck, eventually reconnecting with his ex-wife (Inez) and son (Percy). INEZ
Deadpool 2016 A red-masked hero (Deadpool), his lover (Vanessa), and his nerdy sidekick (Weasel) fight against a man (Ajax) who performed experiments on him. WEASEL
Into the Woods 2014 A witch (The Witch) cast a spell on a man (The Baker) and his wife (The Baker’s Wife), making them unable to have children. THE BAKER
Scary Movie 2000 A teenager (Cindy) fights against a ghost-faced figure (Ghostface) who wields a knife. CINDY CAMPBELL
X-Men 2000 A man in a wheelchair (Charles) starts up a school for mutants. The symbols represent Wolverine, Cyclops, and Storm. CHARLES XAVIER

The last screenshot only contains a single message with 7 distinct emoji, each repeated a number of times. Each of these emoji is also a character in the well-received film The Emoji Movie, and was voiced by an actor who played one of the 7 characters above.

Emoji Number Voice actor (and role in The Emoji Movie) Character Index number into character
Ice cream cone 2 Rob Riggle (played an ice cream emoji) MR WALTERS R
Unimpressed face 2 TJ Miller (played Gene Meh, a “meh” emoji) WEASEL E
Princess 4 Anna Faris (played Jailbreak, who is revealed to be a princess emoji) CINDY CAMPBELL D
Pile of poop 2 Patrick Stewart (played Poop, a poop emoji) CHARLES XAVIER H
Dancer 3 Sofia Vergara (played Flamenca, a flamenco dancer emoji) INEZ E
Hand 5 James Corden (played Hi-5, a hand emoji) THE BAKER A
Smile 8 Maya Rudolph (played Smiler, a smiley emoji) LILLIAN DONOVAN D

Indexing the number of each emoji into the other character played by its voice actor gives the answer REDHEAD.

Author’s Notes

Rob Riggle’s role in The Emoji Movie is actually uncredited, but I ended up using him because I couldn’t find good movie roles with unique emoji symbols for the other main characters (e.g. I could only use one of the Meh family), and he is still listed on Wikipedia. An earlier version of this puzzle used Sean Hayes’s role in The Three Stooges in place of 21 Jump Street, but it got scrapped when no one ID’d it in testsolving, as it was very difficult to clue both the title and plot of that film with emoji.

This answer was sort of assigned arbitrarily to the puzzle, but while doing research, I found out that redhead emojis notably don’t exist yet but are slated to become an emoji later this year, so it turned out to be pretty fitting.
