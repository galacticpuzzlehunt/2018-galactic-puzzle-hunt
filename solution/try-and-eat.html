Solution: Try and Eat
Answer: SKINNY

Written by Ben Yang

This puzzle is about creating 6-letter words by placing one 3-letter word inside another 3-letter word. An example would be using TRY and EAT from the title to create TREATY.

Looking at the first letters of the 6-letter words (the Eaters and Items Eaten sections are alphabetized), we get the clue phrase CLOUD PLACE EATS HOTEL. From this, we find the 3-letter words SKY and INN, which when combined give the final answer of SKINNY.

Author’s Notes

This puzzle's idea was pretty simple and straightforward: “notice property about an answer word”, “find other words with this property”, then “combine using common puzzle techniques”.

There were more words fitting this pattern than I expected, so I tried imposing the additional constraint of wanting each of the 3-letter words to be nouns. This worked out since the first letters with limited pools (the vowels, mostly) didn't really lose any options with this restriction. Some options like EG[GIN]G were considered but excluded due to the similarity between the full word and one of its components.

The cluephrase for this puzzle went through a few revisions because SKY is surprisingly hard to clue well. Originally it was clued by ATMOSPHERE but that didn't feel like that good a match. STRATOSPHERE was considered, but then during testsolving we found out that there's a hotel in Las Vegas called the Stratosphere. Eventually, the clue ended up as CLOUD PLACE.
