Solution: Tabloid News
Answer: GOLDWYN

Written by Rahul Sridhar
Art by Lewis Chen

Each of the headlines references a figure at the center of a certain scandal ending in “-gate” (bridgegate, deflategate) and a word that ends in “gate” (conjugate, variegate). The scandal and the word are always the same length and overlap in exactly one letter. These letters read in order produce the clue phrase SCANDAL ACTOR TONY which yields the answer GOLDWYN.
